From Editor's Desk

My wishes...





First and foremost we, on behalf of the editorial board of the SPECTRUM would like to offer our gratitude to the respected Professors, all the authors and the readers and wish a wonderful year ahead.

When a thought that has been enduring in mind becomes real, it is truly an interesting and exciting experience. This new assignment of ours as Editor of the 14th edition of the SPECTRUM is one such cherished work which we have tried to fulfill to the best of our abilities.

It is worth mentioning that 2020 was such a year which had created an immense loss in our academics, and as such, publishing the publishing the bulletin within such short period of time inspite of the hectic schedule was an uphill task. We specially would like to thank our respected HoD madam Dr. Bandana Das, and all the professors-in-charge without whose valuable support and guidance such a difficult task would have been near to impossible. Also, credit goes to all the other members of 5th Semester.

At last, we are happy to inform that this year, we have tried to publish an e-SPECTRUM, for the greater benefit of the readers. The website links for the same has been provided on the cover page.

Though we have tried our best to make the bulletin as perfect as possible, but unwantedly some mistakes may have been unrectified, for which we regret.

Principal

Pandu College
